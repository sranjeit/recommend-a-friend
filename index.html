Frequently asked questions

Is this completely free?

Yes. There are no fees or charges of any kind to share someone's details or to receive an appreciation benefit.

Will my friend be contacted repeatedly?

No. We will make one respectful contact to share course information. Your friend decides whether they are interested — there is no pressure and no follow-up.

How does the ₹500 appreciation work?

If the person you share this with successfully enrolls in an eligible course, you may qualify for a ₹500 appreciation as a thank-you for sharing the opportunity. This is a gesture of appreciation — not a commission or an income source.

Can my friend share this with someone else?

Yes. If your friend finds the opportunity useful, they can share it with others who may also benefit. Each person gets their own unique link so we can say thank you to the right person.

What happens to the information I share?

The details you provide are used only to contact the person you've recommended with course information, and to reach you if we need to confirm anything. Your information is never sold or shared with third parties.

Privacy & Terms

Privacy notice

We collect only what's needed to act on a recommendation: your name and mobile number, and the name, number and course interest of the person you're recommending. This information is stored in a private, access-controlled system and is never sold or shared with third parties. It is used solely to make contact and, where applicable, to confirm an appreciation benefit.

Appreciation benefit

When a person you recommend successfully enrolls in an eligible course, you may qualify for a ₹500 appreciation as a thank-you for sharing the opportunity. Both sharing and receiving the appreciation are completely free of charge. Eligibility is verified manually and at the program coordinator's discretion.
