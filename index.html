Privacy, terms & how appreciation works

Privacy notice

We collect only what's needed to act on a recommendation: your name and mobile number, and the name, number and course interest of the person you're recommending. This information is stored in a private, access-controlled system and is never sold or shared with third parties. It is used solely to make one respectful contact and, where applicable, to process your appreciation payment.

Consent

By submitting, you confirm you have a genuine, good-faith reason to believe the person you're recommending may be interested in learning about our programs. We rely on your judgment as the referrer — please only recommend people you know.

How the ₹500 appreciation works

If the person you recommend directly is admitted into a program, you become eligible for a ₹500 appreciation payment. There is:

  • No joining fee or membership required to participate
  • No purchase necessary, for you or the person you recommend
  • No reward for anyone your recommended person later refers — appreciation applies to direct recommendations only
  • No multi-level or downline structure of any kind

We track how links are shared for our own analytics — to understand which recommendations lead to genuine interest — but this tracking never creates additional layers of reward. It exists so we can thank the right person, not to build a network.

This is not MLM

Brightpath Institute's referral appreciation is a simple thank-you for a direct, genuine introduction — comparable to an employee referral bonus. It is not a business opportunity, does not involve recruitment into a selling structure, and carries no financial risk or obligation for participants.
