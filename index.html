Privacy, terms & referral benefits

Privacy notice

We collect only what's needed to act on a recommendation: your name and mobile number, and the name, number and course interest of the person you're recommending. This information is stored in a private, access-controlled system and is never sold or shared with third parties. It is used solely to make contact and, where applicable, to process your referral payment.

Consent

By submitting, you confirm you have a genuine reason to believe the person you're recommending may be interested in learning about these programs.

How the ₹500 referral benefit works

When a person you refer successfully registers and enrolls in a course, you become eligible for a ₹500 referral reward. Both submitting referrals and receiving rewards are completely free of charge.
